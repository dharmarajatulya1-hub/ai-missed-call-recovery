Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly does the AI respond after a missed call?
Our AI typically responds within 30-60 seconds of a missed call. Speed is critical because customer interest drops significantly after just a few minutes. The faster we respond, the higher the conversion rate into booked appointments.
What if I'm on another call when someone calls?
That's exactly what we're designed for! The system detects when your line is busy and immediately initiates the recovery process. You can configure it to engage after a certain number of rings or when calls go to voicemail, ensuring every potential customer gets a response.
Can I customize the messages that get sent?
Absolutely! You can customize message templates, tone, branding, and even create different responses based on time of day or call type. Our AI learns your business voice and creates natural, professional messages that sound authentically from your business.
Is there a contract or can I cancel anytime?
No contracts, no commitments. All plans are month-to-month and you can cancel anytime with zero cancellation fees. We're confident you'll see immediate ROI, but if it's not right for your business, you're free to leave at any time.
How do I know if this will work for my type of business?
If you're a local service business that books appointments (dentists, med spas, HVAC, plumbing, legal, etc.) and you miss calls, this will work.
